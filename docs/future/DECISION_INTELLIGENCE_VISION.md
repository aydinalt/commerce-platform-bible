## 1. Purpose

This document captures the long-term vision for Decision Intelligence.

It is not part of the active roadmap and introduces no implementation
commitment.

## 2. Product Philosophy

The platform never makes decisions for users.

The platform helps users complete decisions:

-   Faster
-   Safer
-   More Informed

## 3. Vision

Decision Intelligence is an intelligent decision assistance layer built
on top of the Decision Engine.

AI assists.

Decision Engine orchestrates.

The user decides.

## 4. Core Principle

Decision Engine provides facts.

Decision Intelligence provides context.

AI provides reasoning.

The user owns the final decision.

## 6. AI Provider Independence

Conceptual flow:

Decision Engine

↓

Decision Intelligence

↓

AI Provider Layer

↓

Multiple AI Providers

## 7. AI Usage Principles

AI may:

-   explain
-   summarize
-   compare
-   identify risks
-   identify alternatives

AI must never:

-   make purchasing decisions
-   manipulate users
-   guarantee outcomes

## 8. Future Research

-   Multi-agent reasoning
-   Negotiation assistance
-   Personalized advisors
-   Explainable AI
-   AI-generated buying reports

## 9. Success Metric

> "I feel more confident about my decision."

not

> "The AI made the decision for me."

## 10. Out of Scope

## Closing Statement

Decision Intelligence exists to improve human decision quality.

The platform never replaces human judgment.
