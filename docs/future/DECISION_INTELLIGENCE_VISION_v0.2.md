# Guiding Principle

> The platform never makes decisions for the user.

> The platform helps users complete decisions faster, safer and more
> informed.

Everything described below is exploratory.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Core Architecture Vision

Decision Engine remains the core of the platform.

Decision Intelligence is an advisory layer built on top of the Decision
Engine.

Artificial Intelligence supports Decision Intelligence.

The user always owns the final decision.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Decision Engine

Decision Engine is always the primary source of platform
recommendations.

It uses:

-   Marketplace data
-   Attributes
-   Comparisons
-   Trust indicators
-   Business rules
-   Historical information

AI never replaces Decision Engine.

# AI Principles

AI may:

-   explain
-   compare
-   summarize
-   identify risks
-   suggest alternatives
-   highlight missing information

AI must never:

-   decide for users
-   manipulate purchasing decisions
-   guarantee outcomes
