# Mission

- **Status:** Approved
- **Version:** 1.0

## 1. Mission Statement

Our mission is to help people complete decisions faster while respecting their time. Every day, we work to turn decisions that feel heavy into decisions that feel simple.

## 2. What We Do Every Day

Every day we help people complete decisions faster. We keep the experience simple, preferring simplicity over complexity, and we use AI to assist people as they decide — never to replace human decisions.

## 3. Who We Serve

We serve the people making decisions, and we serve the businesses that meet them there as partners. Our work is done for a user the moment their decision is complete; users should leave the platform after completing their decision.

## 4. How We Create Value

We create value by making decisions clearer and faster to complete:

- The platform is built around a universal Offering model, so many kinds of decisions can live side by side.
- Categories organize content.
- Attributes power discovery, filtering, and comparison.
- Admin provides actionable guidance rather than only reports, helping the platform run well for the people and partners who depend on it.

The platform is designed to scale across many industries, so this value can reach people wherever they have a decision to make.

## 5. Our Daily Commitments

- We respect the user's time.
- We help people complete decisions faster.
- We hold trust as more important than growth.
- We let users leave the platform after completing their decision.
- We use AI to assist, never to replace human decisions.
- We treat businesses as partners.
- We prefer simplicity over complexity.

## 6. Long-Term Commitment

Our commitment is meant to last. We continue to help people decide across many industries, keeping the platform simple as it grows, and choosing trust over growth at every step. The way we work today is intended to remain valid for many years.

## 7. Final Mission Statement

We come to work to help people complete decisions faster and to give them back their time. We serve the people deciding and the businesses that partner with us. We keep things simple, we let AI assist without ever replacing the person deciding, and we value trust over growth. Built around a universal Offering model — organized by categories, powered by attributes, and open to many industries — our mission is to make deciding simpler, day after day, for many years.
